How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App Deletion

You can delete your account directly within the Wayzen app:

  1. Open the Wayzen app
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by sending an email to:

zgh540513@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Wayzen account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account information (email, name, profile photo)
  • Recorded routes and activity sessions
  • Friend connections and social data
  • All location data associated with your account

This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
